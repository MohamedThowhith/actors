Vijay

Vijay, popularly known as Thalapathy Vijay, is one of the most influential actors in the Tamil film industry. Born on June 22, 1974, he is known for his charismatic screen presence, energetic dance moves, and strong connection with fans. Vijay began his career as a child actor and later rose to fame with several blockbuster films such as *Ghilli*, *Thuppakki*, and *Master*. Over the years, he has built a massive fan base not only in Tamil Nadu but across India and internationally. Apart from acting, Vijay is also known for his philanthropic activities and social messages in his films, making him a beloved figure both on and off the screen. 🎬⭐
